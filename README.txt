HOW TO TEST LOCALLY
--------------------
1. Open the "aasuslimited" folder
2. Double-click "index.html" to open in your browser
3. All pages and navigation will work immediately


HOW TO UPLOAD TO YOUR HOSTING
-------------------------------
Upload the entire contents of the "aasuslimited" folder
to your hosting root directory (usually called
"public_html" or "www") using FTP or your hosting
file manager.

Make sure the "images" folder is uploaded too —
without it, photos will not show.
